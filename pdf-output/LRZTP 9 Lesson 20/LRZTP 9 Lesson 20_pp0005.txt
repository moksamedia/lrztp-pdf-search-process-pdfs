Nouns
personal habit

state, as in: གནམ་གཤིས་ = weather
character, personality
གཤིས་ as in: གནམ་གཤིས་ = weather
behaviour, conduct, way of conducting oneself
as in: བེད་སོད་བྱེད་པ་ = to use, གསང་སོད་ = toilet (lit. secret conduct), སོད་འཇུག

a mind which wishes to benefit others

meaning, purpose
དོན་ = meaning, purpose, as in: གཞན་དོན་ = the purpose of others

Verbs

གུས་ཞབས་བྱེད་པ།

to be respectful, to venerate, show one's respect (ཐདད་པ་)
གུས་ = respect; ཞབས་ = leg, foot (H); བྱེད་པ་ = to do, verbalizer
to get angry, to give rise to hatred (syn: ཁོང་ཁོ་ཟ་བ་) (ཐ་མི་དད་པ་)
ཞེ་སྡང་ = anger, as in: དུག་གསུམ་ནི་འདོད་ཆགས་ཞེ་སྡང་གཏི་མུག = the three
poisons, attachment, anger and ignorance; ལངས་པ་ = to arise
to give rise to attachment, to have desire (ཐ་མི་དད་པ་)
འདོད་པ་ = to want, to desire; ཆགས་ = attachment; ལངས་པ་ = to arise
