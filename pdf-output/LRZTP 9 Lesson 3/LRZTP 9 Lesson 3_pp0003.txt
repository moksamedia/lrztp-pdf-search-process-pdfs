There are lots of domestic animals where I come from. For example there are many

What are these big animals?
Don't you know? These are dzo. The mother of a dzo is a cow and the father a yak.
Oh, I see. Really? There are no dzo in my country. This is our city. In this city, there
are many cars and super-high buildings.
Well then, your city and my country are not the same! In our valley there are only
monasteries and animals.
