Important note regarding usage: There are occasions where "can" and "could" are applicable
in English, but where it would be incorrect to use gq" in Tibetan. For example:

A literal translation of the above would be: "Do I have the physical strength to open the
door?" The correct way to form the questions, "Can I do X?” / “Could you do X?" etc. is as
