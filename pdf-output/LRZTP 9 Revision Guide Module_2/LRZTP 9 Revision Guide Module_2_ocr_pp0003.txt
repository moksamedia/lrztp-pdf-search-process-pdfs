Only
..-Remember that... There are three ways of saying ‘only’ in Tibetan: using གཅིག་པུ་, OR, མ་གཏོགས་
The first one literally means ‘alone’ and the verb is not negated. With aay and མ་གཏོགས་ the verb is
negated since they mean: འལའད།མང་བ།་ or: ‘apart from’.

ངའི་ཅོ་ཅོ་གཅིག་གཅིག་པུ་ནང་ལ་བརྲད་ཡོད།

Only my big brother is at home.

He/she has only 20 rupees.
