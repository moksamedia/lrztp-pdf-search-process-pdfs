6) A word about the word order and the emphasis in a Tibetan sentence
a)

The verb stays at the end

b) Whatever stays next to the verb is emphasised if it is not the common sentence order.
Usual structure, no particular emphasis:
འདི་སྨུག་གུ་ནག་པོ་རེད།

This is a black pen.

རྒྱུག་གུ་འདི་ནག་པོ་རེད།

This pen is black.

Special emphasis - less common but used if you want to emphasise something!

c)

སྨྱུག་གུ་ནག་པོ་འདི་རེད།

This is the black pen! Or: The black pen is this!

ནག་པོ་འདེ་སྒྱུག་གུ་རེད།

This black (thing) is a pen.

ནག་པོ་སྨུག་གུ་འདི་རེད།

This pen is the black one! (not that one!)
