These seemingly unimportant little syllables between words are actually crucial. They define
the function and relationships of words within a sentence. The Tibetan word for “particle” is
ཕྲད་, which means “to meet.”

DEPENDENT AND INDEPENDENT PARTICLES
All particles fall into one or other of the following two categories – dependent and independent.
ཕྲད་གཞན་དབང་ཅན། Dependent particles
Dependent refers to the fact that a single particle has various spellings which depend on the
(gender of the) suffix of the preceding word. For example:
ཁེད་རང་གི་མིང་
your name, name of you
