LRZTP 9 Module 3 – Lesson 26, December 2023
པུ་གུ་འདིས་རྐང་འཁོར་ཡག་པོ་རང་གཏོང་ཤེས་ཀི་མེད་ཙང་རིལ་འགོའོ་བེད་ཀི་འདུག
Because this child doesn’t know how to ride his bicycle well, he keeps almost falling off.
A subtlety: རིལ་འགོའོ་བེད་ཀི་འདུག carries the notion that this action of almost falling is still in process,
whereas རིལ་འགོའོ་འདུག would simply mean that the child is about to fall (once only).
