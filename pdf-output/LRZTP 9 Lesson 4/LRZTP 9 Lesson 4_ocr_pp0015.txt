Ill. Whatever stands next to the verb is crucial.
Apart from the two rules above, people have different opinions on whether there is a standardized
word order in Tibetan or not.
Students of the Tibetan language tend to hold the position that “subject — object / adjective —
verb” is the “usual” order, while Tibetans themselves would probably say that there is no such
“common”

order; that the word order depends entirely on the context. Both agree however that
