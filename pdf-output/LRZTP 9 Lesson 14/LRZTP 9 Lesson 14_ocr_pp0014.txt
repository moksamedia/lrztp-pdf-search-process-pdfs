So, according to the formula, the sentence is constructed like this:

Because Lobsang is Tibetan (reason), he drinks lots of Tibetan tea (/7©57///).
This causal relationship could equally be expressed in two sentences, in which case the
second one would start with gars (therefore):

བློ་བཟང་བོད་པ་རེད། བྱས་ཙང་ཁོང་བོད་ཇ་མང་པོ་འཐུང་གི་ཡོད་རེད།
Lobsang is (a) Tibetan. Therefore he drinks a lot of Tibetan tea.
