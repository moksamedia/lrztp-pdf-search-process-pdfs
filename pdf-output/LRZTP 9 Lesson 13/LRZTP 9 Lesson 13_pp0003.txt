Buddhism
at someone's, at someone's house, close, nearby
རྩ་ = root, vein, as in: རྩ་བའི་བླ་མ་ = root lama
place to stay, home, house

place of the gods, སེ་ས་ = birth place, ས་ཆུ་མེ་རླུང་ནམ་མཁའ་ = [the
elements] earth, water, fire, air, space
place to stay, home, house (H)
བཞུགས་པ་ = to stay, to sit, to live (H), as in: ག་ལེ་བཞུགས་ = “’Bye!”; ས་
= earth, place, as in: ལྷ་ས་ = Lhasa, the place of the gods
room, chamber, compartment, cell

schedule

room mate
house owner, landlord
ཁང་པ་ = room, house; བདག་ = self, as in: བདག་གཞན་ = self and other,
བདག་པོ་ = owner, བདག་སྒྲ་ = Tibetan grammatical term for the

neighbour
