Here, the first བྱེད་སྒྲ་, namely གིས་, marks the main agent of the action - Lobsang.
The second བྱེད་སྒྲ་, namely w added to the word ལག་པ, indicates the subsidiary agent, the instrument of the action his hands.
As illustrated by earlier examples, most sentences only have a main agent བྱེད་པ་པོ་གཙོ་བོ།.
