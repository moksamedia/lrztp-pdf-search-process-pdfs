Negative comparisons
Negative comparisons are not used very often, but are formed as follows:

མོག་མོག་འདེ་ཚོ་ཁེ་ས་ལས་ཞིམ་པ་མི་འདུག
These momos are less tasty than [the ones] yesterday.

སྒུང་འདི་དེབ་གཞན་དག་དེ་ཚོ་ལས་སྙན་པ་མི་འདུག

This story is less interesting [to read] than those other books.
