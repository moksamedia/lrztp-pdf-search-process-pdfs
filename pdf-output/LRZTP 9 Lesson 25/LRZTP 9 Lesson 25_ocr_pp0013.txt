རྩིས་པ། (to plan, to consider)

རྩིས་པ། is mostly used in the Present Perfect with the auxiliaries ཡོད།, མེད།, ཡོད་རེད། and ཡོད་མ་རེད།
The testimonial form is not commonly used because one cannot have direct experience of someone
else's plans. There is no Future conjugation in this case, since current plans anyway tend to carry
some future connotation. For example:
