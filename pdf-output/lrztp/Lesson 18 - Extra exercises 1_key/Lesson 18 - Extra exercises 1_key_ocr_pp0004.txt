There are many sacred pilgrimage sites to visit in Nepal
There are three great stupas in the capital city.
The biggest stupa is said to be the

Jarung Khashor (Boudha Stupa).

At this stupa there are lots of people doing circumnambulations, prostrations and turning
prayer wheels.
