this point. One can read this sentence as either: “Do you have (at this time) the action of

Since the question would not be necessary if the speaker sits in that moment with the

Examples with Explanations
In order to be able to actively use present tense, you just need to apply the same logic and
systems as seen in the context of the past tense.
དེང་སང་ང་ད་ྲྭ རྒྱ་མང་པོ་སྔོག་གི་མེད། ཡིན་ནའི་ལྟད་མོ་མང་པོ་ལྟ་གི་ཡོད།
These days, I do not surf the internet much. But I watch a lot of movies.
The auxiliaries གི་ཡོད། གི་མེད། which pertain to བདག express that the action is done with
intention.
ཁེད་རང་གོག་བརྙན་ཁང་ལ་མང་པོ་འགྲོ་གི་ཡོད་པས།
Do you go to the cinema often?
The auxiliary གི་ཡོད་པས། again shows volition and also turns it into a question.
ཁོང་ཚོ་ད་ལྟ་ངལ་གསོ་རྒྱག་གི་འདུག་གས།1
Are they taking rest now?
The auxiliary གི་འདུག་གས།, apart from making it into a question, also expresses that the person
who is asking assumes that the person asked has a direct knowledge of the answer:
ལགས་མི་འདུག ཁོང་ཚོ་ད་ལྟ་ཕག་འཚལ་གི་འདུག
No, they are prostrating at the moment.
The auxiliary གི་འདུག expresses that the speaker has direct experience of the information.
ཁོང་ཚོ་གཟའ་མཇུག་ལ་གིང་ག་གཏོང་གི་ཡོད་རེད་པས།
Do they have picnic in the weekend(s)?
The auxiliary གི་ཡོད་རེད་པས། can have two interpretations here, but as mentioned before, it is
more likely to mean “Do they “usually” have picnic in the weekends?”
