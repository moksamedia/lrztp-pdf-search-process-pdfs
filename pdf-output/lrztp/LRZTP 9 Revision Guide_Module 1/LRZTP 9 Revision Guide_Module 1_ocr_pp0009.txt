༠) ཐ་དད་པ་

ཐ་མི་དད་པ་ + བྱེད་སྒྲ་ Agentive Particle

All verbs are either ཐ་དད་པ་ ༠ ཐ་མི་དད་པ་ - 3 concept similar to "transitive" and "intransitive". Verbs
of motion (འགྲོ་ to go, Mic" to come, ཕེབས་ to go, come (H), བསྡད་ to stay, live, sit) are ཐ་མི་དད་པ་ and in

འཁྱག་པ་ to feel cold etc.) also.
ཐ་དད་པ་ verbs require the use of a བྱེད་སྒྲ་ (Agentive particle) in the past.
You need to be able to identify the བྱེད་སྒྲ་ in a given past tense sentence and then use it in the answer.
For example:
If I write:

Of course! I wrote a little bit of homework.
or, - honestly ལགས་མེད། ངས་རང་སྦྱོང་བྲིས་མེད། No, I did not write (my) homework.
