 when you say Muslim ཁ་ཆེ་, you are actually saying
“big mouth.” It is because a devoted Muslim prays
five times a day.
 when you say Buddhist ནང་པ་, you are actually
saying “insider” or “the ones who look inside”
 when you say India རྒྱ་གར་, you are saying
“the white vast (land)” (རྒྱ་ = vast, broad;
གར་ comes from དཀར་པོ་ = white.)
