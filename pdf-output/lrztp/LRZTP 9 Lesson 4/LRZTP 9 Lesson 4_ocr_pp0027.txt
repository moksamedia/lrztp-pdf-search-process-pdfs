Expressing Wondering
There is one more option how to ask a question which is commonly used. Namely, instead of
asking straightforwardly:

ཁྱེད་རང་ལུང་པ་ག་ནས་ཡིན། or: ཁྱེད་རང་ལུང་པ་ག་ནས་རེད།
Which country are you from?
You may choose to say:
ཁྱེད་རང་ལུང་པ་ག་ནས་ཡིན་ན།
I wonder which country you are from.
It seems a bit more polite than the other ways because it is not an actual question. Since it
expresses a personal wondering and not an objective question about an external fact, it is
always the auxiliaries pertaining to བདག་ which are used with this way of questioning and

never རེད་ན། འདུག་ན།
