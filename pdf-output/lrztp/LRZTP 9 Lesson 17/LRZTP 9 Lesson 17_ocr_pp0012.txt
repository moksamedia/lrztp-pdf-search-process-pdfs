Note: As with the comparative, the objects of comparison in the superlative mode are not always
explicitly mentioned:
ཐུགས་སྤྲོ་ལ་རོགས་པ་དང་མཉམ་དུ་འགྲོ་ན་སྐྱིད་ཤོས་རེད།
It is most fun to go to a party with friends.
(as opposed to going alone or with one's brother or sister, etc.)
As stated above, if one is at the moment of discovering or has just discovered a superlative, the
auxiliary འདུག or རེད་འདུག is used:
ཤིང་ཏོག་ཚང་མའི་ནང་ནས་ཚ་ལ་མ་གོང་ཁེ་ཤོས་རེད་འདུག

[I am now just seeing that] oranges are the cheapest of all the fruits.
