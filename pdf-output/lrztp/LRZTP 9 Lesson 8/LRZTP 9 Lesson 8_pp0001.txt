Lesson 8 About Eating and Cooking

Objectives
1. You will be able to talk about various kinds of food in the three times, including
people’s preferences and habits, ordering it at the restaurant, and how to prepare a dish of
your choice.
2. You will be able to discuss upcoming actions and plans: what you or somebody
else will do in the near and far future. And you can express your wish to volunteer to do
something for the sake, or instead, of somebody else.
