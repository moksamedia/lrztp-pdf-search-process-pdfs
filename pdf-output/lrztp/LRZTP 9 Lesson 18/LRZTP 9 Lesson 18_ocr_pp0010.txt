b) Nominalizer =y
The nominalizer *y has various functions, but in this lesson only the simplest of these is introduced.

“The fact that...”
Here, 4 is added to the past tense of the verb and 18 translated as “that” or "the fact that." For example:

དེ་རིང་ཁྱེད་རང་འདེར་ཕེབས་པ་དེ་ཡག་པོ་ཞེ་དྲགས་རེད།
It is great that you came here today!
