The four places that mark the stages of the life of Buddha Shakymuni are
important pilgrimage sites:

ane (Lumbini) his birthplace
རྔོ་རྗེ་གདན་ (Bodhgaya) where he attained enlightenment
ཝ་ར་ཌ་སི་ (Varanasi, Sarnath) where he first turned the of wheel of Dharma
ཆུ་མཆོག་གྲོང་ (Kushinagar) where he passed into nirvana

བསོད་ནམས་གསོག་པ།
One of the main purposes of pilgrimage is
བསོད་ནམས་གསོག་པ་ (to accumulate merit).
This is achieved by doing སྐོར་ར་ (circumambulations).

Followers of the Bon tradition circumambulate holy sites anti-clockwise.
From a Buddhist perspective, this is considered སྐོར་ལོག (wrong circumambulation).
