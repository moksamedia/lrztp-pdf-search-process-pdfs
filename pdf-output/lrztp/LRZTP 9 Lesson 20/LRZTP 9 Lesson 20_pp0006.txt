to give rise to stinginess, miserliness (syn: སེར་སྣ་བྱེད་པ་) (ཐ་མི་དད་པ་)
སེར་སྣ་ = stinginess, miserliness; ལངས་པ་ = to arise
to be disappointed, to become disappointed (ཐ་མི་དད་པ་)
བོ་ = mind, as in: བོ་བཟང་ = kind hearted, minded
to argue, to fight (ཐ་དད་པ་)
འཁྲུག་པ་ = to be agitated, disturbed, fight; རྒྱག་པ་ = to do, verbalizer

Adjectives

ཡ་རབས།

excellent, well behaved, virtuous, gentle, respectful
as in: ཡ་རབས་སོད་བཟང་ = excellent, good behaviour
badly behaved, malicious, pertaining to lower classes of beings
མ་ = negative; རབས་ = race, lineage, as in: མི་རབས་ = generation, དུས་
རབས་ = century
tolerant, patient, having a forbearing nature, of good character
ངང་ = character, disposition; རྒྱུད་ = stream, mental continuum, as in:
བཀའ་རྒྱུད་ = Kagyu; རིང་པོ་ = long, as in: ཚེ་རིང་ = long life
patient, tolerant
བཟོད་པ་ = patience, forbearance, to forbear; ཆེན་པོ་ = great
reliable, trustworthy
བོ་ = mind; འཁེལ་བ་ = to trust, to rely, to lean upon
firm, stable, good
བརྟན་ = stable, as in the Tibetan name: རབ་བརྟན་ Rabten
gets angry very easily, short tempered
རླུང་ = air, wind, anger; ཚ་པོ་ = hot
kind hearted, kind, friendly, lovely
སེམས་པ་ = heart, mind; བཟང་པོ་ = good, positive, as in: ཚེས་བཟང་དུས་
བཟང་ = special dates and day in the Tibetan lunar calendar
diligent, persevering, rejoicing in virtue
བརོན་འགྲུས་ = diligence; ཆེན་པོ་ = big, great
