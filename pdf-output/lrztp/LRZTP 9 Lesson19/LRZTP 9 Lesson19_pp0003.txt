བོད་པའི་ལོ་གསར།

Tibetan New Year
བོད་ = Tibet; ལོ་ = year; གསར་ = new, as in: ཚིག་གསར་ = new words, vocabulary
auspiciousness, good connection
རྟེན་པ་ = to depend on, to be related, dependent; འབེལ་བ་ = connection, as in:
འབེལ་ས་ = connective particle
customs, traditions, customary way of doing something
ལུགས་ = system, mode, way, as in: ཆོས་ལུགས་ = religious tradition, religious
belief; སོལ་ = customary way of doing x, nominalizer
puja where tormas are offered on the 29th day of the 12th Tibetan month
དགུ་ = 9, as in: ཉི་ཤུ་རྩ་དགུ་ = 29; གཏོར་བ་ = to scatter, smash, break
service, ritual, puja
ཞབས་ = foot, leg (H); བརྟན་ = བརྟན་པོ་ = firm, stable
the soup with nine ingredients, to be drunk on the 29th day of the month
དགུ་ = 9; ཐུག = soup, as in: འཐེན་ཐུག = handmade noodle soup
wool
as in: བལ་ཡུལ་ = Nepal, lit. land of the wool
prayer flag
རླུང་ = wind, air; རྟ་ = horse
salt (not to be confused with: ཚ་ = hot)
