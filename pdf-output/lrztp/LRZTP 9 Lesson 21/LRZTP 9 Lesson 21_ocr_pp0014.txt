ཞོར་ལ།
ཞོར་ལ་ has a connotation that is slightly different from that of the other temporal connectives. Here,
a single agent performs two actions — one being the main action and the other being subsidiary. The
nominalizer and connective particle (པའི་ཞོར་ལ།) are added to the verb denoting the main action. The
most common usage of ཞོར་ལ་ is to be added to the verb འགོ་, in which context it is translated as “‘on

the way” / “on one’s way.” For example:

ང་འཛིན་གྲྭ་ལ་ཕྱིན་པའ་ཞོར་ལ་སྐེར་ར་ཁ་ཤས་རྒྱག་གེ་ཡེད།
