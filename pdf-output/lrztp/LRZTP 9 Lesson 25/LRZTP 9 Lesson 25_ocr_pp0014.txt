ཆོག་ is most commonly used to talk about the Present but is occasionally also used to describe the
Past. Since being allowed to do something (or not) always depends on outer conditions (གཞན་)› the
auxiliaries གི་རེད། anda} v5)

are most commonly used. (See Lesson 7 for an explanation of how
