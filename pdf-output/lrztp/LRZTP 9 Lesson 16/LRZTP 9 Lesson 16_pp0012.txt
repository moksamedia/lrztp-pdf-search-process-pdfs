warm, lukewarm
དོད་ = heat; འཇམ་ = gentle, soft, as in: འཇམ་དཔལ་དབྱངས་ = Manjushri
bad, ugly, tough (also spelled: སྡུག་ཆག)
སྡུག = unpleasant, as in: སྡུག་བསྔལ་ = suffering
high
མཐོ་ = high, as in: མཐོ་རིམ་སོབ་གྲྭ་ = university
low
nutritious, བཅུད་= nutrients, content, essence; ཆེན་པོ་= big, great
