LRZTP 9 Module 1 – Lesson 10, July 2023

དེ་རིང་གཟའ་ག་རེ་རེད། What day is it today?

Objectives
1. You will be able to use numbers and some of their aspects, such as dates - both
international and Tibetan calendar - asking and replying “what’s the time?”,
numerals, divisions, etc.
2. You will be able to discuss about people’s plans for this week, month and year,
somebody’s daily schedule including times, weekdays, people’s birthdays, etc.
