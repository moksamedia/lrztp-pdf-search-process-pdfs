In the case of the attached ར་, the ར་བཏགས་, the pronunciation is a bit tricky.
• When it is attached to the main letters of the 1st row (ཀ་,ཁ་,ག་), 2nd row (ཏ་,ཐ་,ད་) and
3rd row (པ་,ཕ་,བ་), the same unusual sound is produced. For example: ཀྲ་ sounds the
same as ཏྲ་ which sounds the same as པྲ་.

changes their low tone into high tone.
