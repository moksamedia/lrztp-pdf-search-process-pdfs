DID YOU KNOW THAT...?
གཟིམ་འཇགས་གནང་གློ
When you say “Good night”
you are actually saying:
“Sleep tightly”.
This syllable འཇགས་ also appears in the
word ལིང་འཇགས་, which means
“calm, peaceful, tranquil.”
