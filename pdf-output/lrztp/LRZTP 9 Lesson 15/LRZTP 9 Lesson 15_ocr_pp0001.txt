སློབ་ཚན་བཅོ་ལྔྫ་པ། — Lesson 15
ལས་ཀ་འདྲ་མིན་སྣ་ཚོགས་ཀྱི་སྐོར། — Various kinds of work
After having studied this lesson...
1.

You will be able to name a large number of common jobs as well as the ones of the
members of your larger family. You will also have the tools to discuss a bit about
their level of difficulty, benefit, salary, responsibility, frequency in your country,
importance, and express your personal preference about them, including reason.

2.

Making a distinction of evidence, you will be able to speculate, make guesses and
express the probability of beings and events related to familiar topics, such as
weather, food, travelling, clothing and rooms, using the four different types of
auxiliaries of probability in the three times, including their corresponding adverbs.
