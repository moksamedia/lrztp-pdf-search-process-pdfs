A Hint: When one thinks in terms of the apostrophe, one will get the word order correct. For example:
བཀྲ་ཤིས་
ཀི་ དེབ།
Tashi
's
book.
Tashi's book
Whereas if one thinks in terms of “the book of Tashi,”- one may end up saying:
*དེབ་
ཀི་ བཀྲ་ཤིས་
Book
of Tashi
- which is, of course, incorrect in Tibetan.

 Personal pronoun + འབེལ་ས་ = possessive pronoun

me + འབེལ་ས་ dog

 Linking a place and a noun
