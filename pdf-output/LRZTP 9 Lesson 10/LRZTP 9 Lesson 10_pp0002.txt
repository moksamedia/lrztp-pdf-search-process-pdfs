DID YOU KNOW THAT...?
ལོ་རྟགས་དང་གནས་སྐོར།
...some of the Tibetan ལོ་རྟགས་ (birth signs),
such as སེ་ (monkey), རྟ་ (horse) and བྱ་ (rooster)
have a particular pilgrimage place which is recommended
to visit in that year.
So, in the སེ་ལོ་ people visit places related to
གུ་རུ་རིན་པོ་ཆེ་ (Padmasambhava),
in the རྟ་ལོ་ there are a lot of pilgrims visiting
གངས་རིན་པོ་ཆེ་ (Mount Kailash)
whereas in the བྱ་ལོ་, it is particularly popular to visit
བལ་ཡུལ་ (Nepal).
Tibetans consider Wednesday to be a special day:
ལྷག་དཀར་ “White Wednesday”.
(ལྷག་ = གཟའ་ལྷག་པ་ = Wednesday;
དཀར་ = དཀར་པོ་ = white)
It is considered one of the བླ་གཟའ་ ("soul" days)
of His Holiness the XIV Dalai Lama.
In order to keep alive the use of ཕྱུ་པ་ (chupa), he encourages Tibetans in exile to
wear Tibetan dress every Wednesday.
