From Shantideva’s Bodhisattvacaryavatara:
There is not a single thing
which does not become easier with familiarization.
Looking at the large and complex scope of this first lesson, please don’t lose heart.
Learning how to read and write Tibetan is just like any other skill: even though it might
look difficult at first, it will become easy with habituation and practice.
