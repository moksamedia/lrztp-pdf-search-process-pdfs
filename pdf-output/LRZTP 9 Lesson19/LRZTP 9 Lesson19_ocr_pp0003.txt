བོད་པའི་ལོ་གསར་རྱི་སྐོར། — Tibetan New Year

མིང་ཚིག

Nouns

བོད་པའི་ལོ་གསར།

Tibetan New Year
བོད་ = Tibet; & = year; གསར་ = new, as in: ཚིག་གསར་ =- new words, vocabulary

རྟེན་འབྲེལ།

auspiciousness, good connection
རྟེན་པ་ = to depend on, to be related, dependent; འབྲེལ་བ་ = connection, as in:

agers: = connective particle
ལུགས་སྲོལ།

customs, traditions, customary way of doing something
ལུགས་ = system, mode, way, as in: ཆོས་ལུགས་ = religious tradition, religious
belief; §er = customary way of doing x, nominalizer

དགུ་གཏོར།

puja where tormas are offered on the 29" day of the 12" Tibetan month
དགུ་ = 9, as in: ཉི་ཤུ་ཅ་དགུ་ = 29; གཏོར་བ་ = to scatter, smash, break

ཞབས་བཏན།

service, ritual, puja

ཞབས་ = foot, leg (H); བརྟན་ = བརྟན་པོ་ = firm, stable
དགུ་ཐུག

the soup with nine ingredients, to be drunk on the 29th day of the month
དྭགུ་ ༤ 9; ཐུག = soup, as 111: འཐེན་ཐུག = handmade noodle soup

པལ།

wool
as in: Raryyar = Nepal, lit. land of the wool

ཟླུང་རྟ།

prayer flag
aN = wind, air; 5 = horse
